Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, educational background, and resume details.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our services, including job searches, applications, and interactions with employers.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To match you with relevant job opportunities
  • To enable employers to find suitable candidates
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about job opportunities, updates, and security alerts
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of ChakriBakri, our users, or others

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With employers when you apply for jobs or when your profile matches their requirements
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • In connection with the sale, merger, or acquisition of all or part of our business

Your resume and profile information will only be visible to employers you apply to or those who purchase access to our student database. You can control your privacy settings in your account dashboard.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption, access controls, and secure servers.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and update your personal information through your account settings
  • Delete your account and personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control the visibility of your profile to employers
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activities and to remember your preferences. These help us provide, protect, and improve our services.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our services.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
